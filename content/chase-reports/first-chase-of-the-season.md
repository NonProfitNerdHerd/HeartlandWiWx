## Forecast

We watched a modest severe setup develop across southern Wisconsin with enough shear and instability to justify the drive.

## On the Road

The Silverado rolled out mid-afternoon with radar open, snacks packed, and a rough target near the Iowa border.

## What We Saw

We intercepted a high-based supercell with dramatic structure, frequent lightning, and a wall cloud that never quite organized into a tornado.

## Lessons Learned

Patience matters. The best structure arrived after the first impulse passed, and staying flexible with the target kept us in position safely.

## Next Time

We will arrive earlier, scout more escape routes, and keep the camera batteries closer to the door.
